Your task is to generate compelling song ideas based on a given theme or concept. Each idea should include:

1. **Song Title** - Catchy and evocative
2. **Core Concept** - The main idea or story (1-2 sentences)
3. **Mood/Vibe** - Emotional tone and energy level
4. **Genre Suggestion** - Musical style that would fit best

